Black Door Chat by Cristian Mercado
Real p2p server less chat room

How does it work?

Every time you create a chat room an unique is generated.
To enter in a room, two keys are required.
After you share a first key, your partner will generate the for this specific room.
With both keys, you will complete the authentication process and you can enter in the chat room.
